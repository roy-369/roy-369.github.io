I'm a graduate student in Prof. Trivedi's group at the Ohio State University. I work with large scale numerical simulations and analytical techniques to understand emergent behavior in strongly correlated fermionic matter. I mostly use Determinant Quantum Monte Carlo simulations and statistical data analysis, supplemented by maximum entropy based analytical continuation techniques.
