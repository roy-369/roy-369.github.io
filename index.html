I'm a graduate student at Dr. Trivedi's group in the Ohio State University. I work with large scale numerical simulations and analytical techniques to understand emergent behavior in strongly correlated fermionic matter. I mostly use Determinant Quantum Monte Carlo simulations and statistical data analysis, supplemented by maximum entropy based analytical continuation techniques.
